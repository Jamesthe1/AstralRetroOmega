# Art Rules

- Light always comes from the top-left of the texture. If it is an entity or block-entity, top-left and behind when facing the player.
- All textures use the palette provided at the root of this repository.
- A material can have no more than 5 colors: main color, light, highlight, shadow, deep shadow. Multiple materials may show up on a given texture.
- Crops must only show a distinct color when they are harvestable.
- Most textures have a smoothness to them by a reduced intensity of dithering.
